About Our Organization

Driven by Compassion, Built on Community

Encouragement by Empowerment exists to serve with integrity, care, and practical support that helps people move from survival to stability and growth.

Our Story

We began as a small volunteer effort offering encouragement and referrals to families navigating hardship. As needs grew, we expanded into structured mentorship, community workshops, and coordinated support services.

Today, we continue to center dignity and hope while building strong relationships with residents, community leaders, and partner organizations.

Mission

To empower lives through encouragement, mentorship, and practical support that fosters long-term wellness, confidence, and opportunity.

Vision

A thriving community where every individual has access to support systems, trusted relationships, and the resources needed to succeed.
